About me

I'm Kunal, Founder & CEO of Orphic India. A young rising Entrepreneur who's experienced in different industries of business like education, health, Transportation & hospitality. also been experienced in managing clients for US & Canada for IT hirings. Started my journey as an Entrepreneur where I was just a student pursing my flight engineering. I was wondering what if one day I'd be having my own business and from my college 2nd year I started learning skills, meeting new people & started knowing about how people are making brands. Associated with different companies for business development and learnings while working with them sometimes it was frustrating and excuse making for me and sometimes it was just glory and gratifying and then understood how amazing it could be as an Entrepreneur. A boss mindset, a boss life challenging everyday to grow more, to take care of your people and your business. Thats when I started pursing it more and then after college, took some experience in aviation field then shifted my job to Human resource management along with my entrepreneurial journey. After a few failures started my own company. Currently growing in transportation and hospitality industry while generating 7 figures revenue every year. As an Entrepreneur my objectives are very clear to take entrepreneurship to a new heights with vision of growth. Where people should be proving jobs rather than seeking for one, To set a benchmark & to inspire other to grow as well. Overall I can say, the journey as an Entrepreneur is just more than an excitement between the fear of failure and a feeling of becoming a boss when there is only you who decides everyday of your life just everything what you want and how you want and that's what decides how bigger or better you grow. where I Rather fail but won't regret.
